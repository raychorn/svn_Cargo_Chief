Cargo Chief Site Spec v1
  • This spec will guide UI development and site design
  • The site will be hosted by a third party, like GoDaddy or Amazon
  • The site will initially handle:
    • 5 simultaneous users
    • 1,000 shippers
    • 1,000 truckers
    • 10,000 bids
    • 10,000 loads
  • The site will back up data hourly or more frequently
  • Would like to have some fail over to another site
  • 77% of websites are PHP, shall we use that http://w3techs.com/technologies/details/pl-php/all/all
  • Simplicity is key, think Hipmunk, Groupon, etc
  • We will want etrust and BBB logos to get folks comfortable with a new site
  • We will want to use HTTPS for the credit card area
  • “Next” is just scrolling
  • Input boxes should be big text 16 or larger font
  • Work on IE 6,7,8; FireFox 4,5,6,7; Safari ?, Chrome?
  • Our blue is PMS 541 (#003f77 ), logo coming, our background is light grey, http://www.color-hex.com/color/ddd8ce, Light grey #2, (#ddd1c1)
  • Top level bar is PMS 541 with white text, second level is PMS 5425 (#8499a5)
  • Good to use black text in white text boxes, hipmunk, apple, groupon
