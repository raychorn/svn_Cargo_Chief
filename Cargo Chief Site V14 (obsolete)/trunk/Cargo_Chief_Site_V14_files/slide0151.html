                             Stops In Transit
Cargo Chief enables the shipper to input additional stops
in between the Origin and the Destination for your quote.
For example, you may have 20 pallets going from Los
Angeles to the Southeast, 6 going to Atlanta and 14 going
Miami.  Miami would be the Destination and Atlanta would
Be a Stop in Transit. 
For shipments with Stops in Transit, Best Quote is used.
